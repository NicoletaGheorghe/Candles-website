About Us

Welcome to The Soothing Sparks, where we believe what creating the perfect ambiance should be simple and fun. What started as a hobby has grown into a passion for crafting beautiful, eco-friendly soy wax candles that fill your home with warmth, comfort and a touch of luxury.

At The Soothing Sparks, we are all about the experience. From selecting the finest 100% soy wax to carefully blending soothing scents, we handcraft each candle with care, ensuring every detail shines. We started out as hobbyists, experimenting with different scents and styles, and now we're proud to share our creations with you. Whether you are looking for a fresh, invigorating fragrance or a calming, cozy scent, we have something for every mood and moment.

Our soy wax candles burn cleaner, longer, and are a more sustainable option for your home. The process we use is as simple and pure as our ingredients, so you can fill good about the candles you bring into your space. With a focus on quality and eco-consciousness, we want each candle to not only brighten your room, but also your day.

Thank you for choosing The Soothing Sparks. We are excited to share our love for this hobby turned passion with you and help you create the perfect atmosphere in your home.
